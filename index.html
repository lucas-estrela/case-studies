Idea 7: Comparisons and Contrasts

While browsing YouTube for Musiversal video reviews, I found one by David Das. He mentioned about his track, that already had recorded drums but wanted to explore something new. To do so, he booked a session with Devin Malloy to create some beats. The result was amazing, he shared, but unfortunately that part wasn't shown. Imagine how impactful it would be to compare these two distinct results?


Using the idea of this example, we can craft a split-screen video, featuring the "before" version on one side and the "after" version on the other. Using captions or voiceover to underscore the disparities and enhancements.


Perhaps even engage the audience by inviting them to voice their preference. This approach also benefits the user/composer, who not only gains visibility on Musiversal's social media but also receives feedback on their track from fellow consumers.
